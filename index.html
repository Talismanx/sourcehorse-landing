Details & Deep-Dive

What it Actually Does

When you click Analyze (or highlight text and analyze), the extension:

  1. Parses the visible article or the selection you highlighted.
  2. Generates a concise summary and extracts key claims.
  3. Returns results in a compact panel—no page reload needed.

Modes

  • Default: Balanced summary + insights.
  • Fact Checker: Emphasis on claims, context, and caveats.
  • Sarcastic: Same analysis with a spicy tone (fun for browsing).
  • Philosopher: Perspective, ethics, & first principles.

Privacy & Security

  • No browsing history is stored by us; a local history tab can keep your recent analyses on your machine.
  • Only the content you explicitly analyze is processed for that session.
  • Stripe handles payments; we never see your card details.

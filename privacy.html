SourceHorse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

SourceHorse values your privacy and is designed with user security in mind. This privacy policy explains how we handle data while providing our AI-powered webpage analysis and fact-checking services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Website Content: When you analyze a page, its text is temporarily sent to our AI servers for processing.
  • We do not collect or store your browsing history.
  • Usage data (like daily analysis count) is stored locally and optionally synced with your SourceHorse account.

2. Account & Authentication

  • Login is handled through Supabase Magic Links using your email address.
  • Your email is used solely for authentication and subscription tracking.
  • We do not share your email or personal information with third parties.

3. Data Usage

  • Page content is processed only to generate your requested AI analysis.
  • No page content or personal data is permanently stored or sold.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Used for authentication and cloud storage of subscription data.
  • Stripe: Used for secure payments and subscription management.
  • Both services comply with industry-standard security and privacy practices.

5. Your Control

  • You can log out or uninstall the extension at any time to remove local data.
  • We do not retain personal data after account deletion.
